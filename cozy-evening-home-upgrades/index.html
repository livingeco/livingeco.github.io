Lighting

Motion Sensor Stair Lights

No more turning on bright overhead lights for late-night trips downstairs.

View Recommended Stair Lights

These small LED strips tuck along the edge of each step and switch on automatically the moment they sense movement, then fade off after you pass. They keep late-night trips to the kitchen or bathroom soft and glare-free, without waking anyone else in the house.

Motion Sensor Cabinet Lights

Find what you need in the kitchen or wardrobe without flooding the room with light.

View Recommended Cabinet Lights

Stick-on LED pucks with a built-in motion sensor light up the inside of a cabinet, pantry shelf, or wardrobe the second you open the door. They run on rechargeable batteries, need no wiring, and quietly make evening routines feel a little more effortless.

Start With One

Where to begin

If I were starting from scratch, I'd begin with motion sensor lighting or soy candles. They're affordable, easy to add, and make the biggest difference on the very first evening. You do not need to do all ten at once. Pick one, notice how it feels, then add the next.
