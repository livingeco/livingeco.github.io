This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the Service. It also explains your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

We use your data only to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Section 01

Interpretation & Definitions

The words and terms below have specific meanings within this Privacy Policy. These definitions apply whether the terms appear in singular or plural form.

Company Refers to Sustainable & Zero Waste Living, also referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our" throughout this document.
Country California, United States.
Device Any device that can access the Service, such as a computer, smartphone, or digital tablet.
Personal Data Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
Service Refers to the Website.
Website The Sustainable & Zero Waste Living website, accessible at livingeco.github.io.
You The individual accessing or using the Service.

Section 02

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information. The only personal data we may collect is your email address, for example through contact forms or newsletter sign-ups.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information such as home addresses, phone numbers, or payment details.

Usage Data

Usage data is collected automatically when you use the Service. This may include information such as your IP address, browser type and version, the pages you visit, time and date of your visit, time spent on each page, your device type, and your operating system.

Section 03

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our Service and to improve user experience. The types of cookies we may use include:

  • Essential Cookies are required for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve it over time.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how some features of this website function.

Section 04

Use of Your Personal Data

We may use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service and ensure it functions correctly for all visitors.
  • To respond to your enquiries, messages, or requests submitted through the site.
  • To improve website functionality and the overall user experience over time.
  • To send occasional updates, eco-living resources, or relevant information, but only if you have opted in to receive them.
  • For analytics purposes, to monitor website performance and understand how the site is used.

Section 05

Retention of Data

We retain collected information only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Data will not be held longer than required unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by applicable law.

Section 06

Disclosure of Data

We may share your data only in the following specific circumstances:

  • With Service Providers who assist us with analytics, website hosting, or performance monitoring. These providers are only given access to the data they need to perform their tasks.
  • For Legal Reasons if we are required to disclose your data by law or in response to valid requests from public or government authorities.
  • With Your Consent when you have actively chosen to share information with us, such as by subscribing to updates or submitting a contact form.

Section 07

Security of Your Data

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet and no method of electronic storage is completely secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can take the necessary steps to remove that information.

Section 09

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites that are not operated by us. If you click on one of these links, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly encourage you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites.

Section 10

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Get in Touch

Questions about this Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please feel free to reach out to us directly.
