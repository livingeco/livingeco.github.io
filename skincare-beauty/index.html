10 Essentials

Your beauty routine is one of the easiest places to cut plastic without giving anything up. Every product here is something you already use every day. These are real swaps, chosen for genuine daily use, proven impact, and strong affiliate value. Start with one and build from there.

Your sustainable beauty journey, in three steps

Step 01 · Start Here The Easiest First Swaps Reusable pads, shampoo bars, conditioner bar, organic soap Step 02 · Upgrade Your Routine Better Products, Less Plastic Natural deodorant, face oil, solid lotion bar, reef-safe sunscreen Step 03 · Finishing Touches Daily Tools That Last for Years Gua sha stone, natural lip balm
Step 01 · Start Here
01
Skincare · Daily Use

Reusable Makeup Remover Pads

Replaces 1000 plus disposable pads Gentle on skin Pays for itself in weeks

Soft, washable cotton rounds that replace the single-use pads most of us reach for every single day without thinking. One set lasts for years and does the job perfectly well for removing makeup, applying toner, or gentle cleansing. This is the single easiest swap you can make in your entire beauty routine and the savings add up fast.

The first swap I recommend to anyone starting a zero-waste beauty routine. Simple, effective, and instantly satisfying.

02
Haircare · Plastic-Free

Shampoo Bar

1 bar outlasts 2 to 3 bottles Zero plastic packaging Travel-friendly

A concentrated, plastic-free bar that lathers well and leaves hair genuinely clean and soft. One bar typically outlasts two or three regular shampoo bottles, which makes it both eco-friendly and much cheaper over time. It is also ideal for travelling because it bypasses liquid restrictions completely and takes up almost no space in a bag.

A plastic-free haircare essential that works from the very first wash. One of the highest-converting swaps in sustainable beauty.

03
Haircare · Zero Waste

Conditioner Bar

Natural pair to the shampoo bar Deeply moisturising No plastic bottle

The natural companion to a shampoo bar, a conditioner bar nourishes and detangles without any plastic packaging at all. Most people who switch to a shampoo bar buy a conditioner bar at the same time, making this one of the best cross-sell opportunities in sustainable beauty. The formulas have improved significantly and leave hair genuinely soft and manageable.

If someone is already buying the shampoo bar, they almost always want this one too. Keep them side by side on the page.

04
Body and Face · Clean

Organic Handmade Soap

No plastic bottle at all Multi-use face and body Clean natural ingredients

A thoughtfully made bar soap with organic oils and plant extracts that is gentle enough for the face yet effective for the whole body. It replaces two or more bottled products at once, comes in minimal or compostable wrapping, and has zero plastic footprint. The lather is rich, the scent comes entirely from natural botanicals, and nothing is wasted.

A versatile daily bar that simplifies the entire shower routine while cutting plastic completely.

Step 02 · Upgrade Your Routine
05
Body · Daily Essential

Natural Deodorant Bar

No aluminium or parabens Zero plastic packaging Enormous search volume

Natural deodorant is one of the most-searched sustainable beauty products in the world right now and for good reason. A deodorant bar eliminates the plastic stick or spray bottle entirely and uses baking soda, shea butter, and plant-based oils to neutralise odour throughout the day. The transition from conventional deodorant takes about a week for most people, after which most never want to go back.

High search intent, daily-use product, strong affiliate conversion. One of the most valuable products on this page.

06
Skincare · Treatment

Organic Face Oil

Rosehip or castor oil Dark glass bottle included Multi-use for face and lashes

A pure, cold-pressed organic oil such as rosehip or castor oil replaces several plastic-packaged serums and treatments at once. Rosehip is rich in vitamin C and naturally brightening. Castor oil supports lash and brow growth and doubles as a deeply nourishing overnight treatment. Both come in small dark glass bottles that protect the oil quality and look beautiful on any bathroom shelf.

A high-value, multi-use product that works for face, lashes, brows, and cuticles. Visitors who discover this rarely stop buying it.

07
Body · Moisturiser

Solid Lotion Bar

Replaces body lotion entirely Zero plastic packaging Concentrated and long-lasting

A solid bar made from shea butter, beeswax, and plant oils that melts on contact with skin and absorbs quickly without any greasy residue. One bar replaces multiple bottles of body lotion and the packaging is either paper or completely naked. Warming it between your hands for a second before applying makes the whole experience feel genuinely luxurious rather than like a compromise.

A satisfying, effective swap that converts well because the product genuinely performs and the eco credentials are obvious.

Shop Now
08
Skincare · Sun Care

Reef-Safe Mineral Sunscreen

No oxybenzone or octinoxate Safe for coral reefs Broad spectrum SPF protection

Most conventional sunscreens contain oxybenzone and octinoxate, two chemicals proven to bleach and destroy coral reefs. A reef-safe mineral sunscreen uses zinc oxide instead, which sits on top of the skin and provides broad spectrum protection without harming marine ecosystems. This is one of the fastest growing categories in sustainable beauty and search volume for reef-safe SPF is climbing sharply in 2026.

Strong eco story, strong health case, and a product that every visitor uses. One of the most shareable product recommendations on this page.

Step 03 · Finishing Touches
09
Skincare · Tool

Gua Sha Facial Stone

Zero waste by nature Lasts a lifetime Trending strongly in 2026

A smooth jade or rose quartz stone used to massage the face, reduce puffiness, improve circulation, and lift facial contours. Gua sha has deep roots in traditional Chinese medicine and has become one of the most widely recommended tools in modern skincare. The stone itself produces zero waste, lasts indefinitely with basic care, and pairs naturally with the face oil above to create a complete daily ritual.

A trending, highly giftable product with genuine wellness benefits. Pairs directly with the face oil to create a complete skincare pairing.

10
Lips · Daily Essential

Natural Lip Balm

Compostable or tin packaging Beeswax and shea butter formula Every person uses this daily

A natural lip balm in a small tin or compostable tube using beeswax, shea butter, and plant oils in place of the petroleum-based formulas most conventional brands rely on. It is deeply nourishing, free from synthetic fragrance, and leaves lips genuinely soft rather than creating a cycle of dependency the way many conventional balms do. The packaging creates zero plastic waste and the price is accessible for almost every visitor.

One of the easiest impulse purchases on this page. Low price, daily use, and zero guilt. A natural add-on to almost any order.
